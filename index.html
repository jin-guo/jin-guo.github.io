I am currently a PhD student in Computer Science at DePaul university. I am interested in utilizing Artificial Intelligent techniques to solve Software Engineering problems. My recent research focuses on mining domain knowledge in the form of ontology from software traceability data and use such knowledge to facilitate automated software engineering tasks such as trace retrieval and project Q&A. Before starting my PhD, I worked in the research lab at Fuji Xerox in areas of image processing and computer vision.
